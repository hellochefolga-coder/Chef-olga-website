Chef Olga

I craft bespoke charcuterie boards and grazing tables that transform any gathering into a sensory experience. Drawing on my Amalfi Coast heritage, I curate abundant spreads of hand selected Mediterranean meats, artisanal cheeses, seasonal fruits, and handcrafted accompaniments, each board a landscape of colour, flavour, and texture.

From intimate dinner parties to grand wedding receptions and corporate events, my boards are designed not just to be eaten, but to be felt. A centrepiece that starts conversations and creates memories.
